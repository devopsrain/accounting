System Information

This accounting software implements double-entry bookkeeping principles:

  • Every transaction affects at least two accounts
  • Total debits must equal total credits
  • Assets = Liabilities + Equity (fundamental accounting equation)
  • Revenue and expenses are tracked separately and closed to retained earnings
🇪🇹 Ethiopian Payroll Features

Integrated Ethiopian payroll system with:

  • Progressive Income Tax (0% to 35%)
  • Pension Contributions: 7% employee, 11% employer
  • Allowances Management: Taxable and non-taxable
  • Automatic Journal Entries for all payroll transactions
  • Pay Slip Generation and reporting
