About Double-Entry Bookkeeping

This system uses double-entry bookkeeping, which means:

  • Every transaction has two sides - a debit and a credit
  • Debits must equal credits in every transaction
  • Assets increase with debits
  • Liabilities & Equity increase with credits
  • Expenses increase with debits
  • Revenue increases with credits
Fundamental Equation:
Assets = Liabilities + Equity
